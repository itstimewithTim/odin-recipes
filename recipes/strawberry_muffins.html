Strawberry Muffins

Description

by Jen Pretty, from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups chopped strawberries
  • 3 teaspoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  • Step 1
    1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
    2. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line with paper liners.
  • Step 2
    1. Beat 3/4 cup sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl using an electric mixer until creamy.
    2. Add egg and mix well.
  • Step 3
    1. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together in a small bowl.
    2. Add flour mixture and milk alternately to butter mixture until combined.
    3. Stir in vanilla extract.
    4. Gently stir in strawberries.
  • Step 4
    1. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups.
    2. Combine 3 teaspoons sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl and sprinkle over tops of muffins.
  • Step 5
    • Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
